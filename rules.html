Crown Network Server Rules

  • 1. **No Cheating** – Use of hacked clients, x-ray, or any unfair advantage is strictly prohibited.
  • 2. **Respect Everyone** – No harassment, racism, or toxicity. Be kind and chill.
  • 3. **No Griefing** – Don't destroy or steal from other players’ builds unless allowed.
  • 4. **No Spam or Advertising** – Keep chat clean. No spam or external promotions.
  • 5. **Use Common Sense** – If you think it’s wrong, it probably is. Play fair!
