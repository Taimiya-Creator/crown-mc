Frequently Asked Questions

Crown Network is a unique Minecraft Lifesteal server where you fight to survive, gaining rewards for every victory. It's a community-driven server where you can show off your skills in combat or simply enjoy the environment.

Joining Crown Network is easy! Simply connect to our server by using the IP: crownnetwork.mc in your Minecraft client.

Simply visit the shop page, choose your items or ranks, and follow the instructions to complete your purchase securely. Payment methods include PayPal, Credit Cards, and more.

If you're interested in becoming a staff member, head over to our staff application page. Fill out the form with your details and experience, and we'll review your application.
